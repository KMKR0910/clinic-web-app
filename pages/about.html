Dr.K.M.Athula Chandrasiri is a highly experienced medical officer with over 30 years of dedicated service in Sri Lanka’s healthcare sector.
A graduate of Univercity of Ruhuna, he has served at prominent hospitals, including Keethumathi Maternity Hospital, Panadura.
Dr. Chandrasiri has played a key role in enhancing healthcare standards across the country. His expertise focuses on providing top-quality care, ensuring the highest standards of diagnosis and treatment.
The medical centre, under his leadership, serves over 1000 patients monthly, offering modern facilities and personalized care.
